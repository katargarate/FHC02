SPACE QUADS ROLLER DISCO

DISCO ON WHEELS

We are proud to present one of the most unique events Graz has to offer: The Space Quads Roller Disco! Join us for a cosmically cool evening we put on in cooperation with the Helmut List Halle. The combination of good music, disco atmosphere and roller skates makes this intergalactic party an unforgettable experience!

OUT OF THIS WORLD

For this special event the venue is transformed into the spaciest disco on this side of the Milky Way! All earthlings are welcome (on and off skates) to wear their most fabulous outfits and costumes. Rental skates are available for those who don’t have their own!

OPEN DISCO

Our Open Disco is for skaters of all ages. Families are welcome to bring their kids, even parents with babies can join in on the fun and bring their strollers onto the rink. It's not every day that our little ones get the chance to experience a classic roller disco!

LATE NIGHT DISCO

After the Open Disco the lights get dimmed, the music gets louder and the Late Night Disco begins! An event for our 18+ guests who want to experience nightlife in a totally different way. Complete with refreshing drinks, snacks and galactic vibes this party has it all!

PAST EVENTS

Get ready to roll - the Space Quads Roller Disco is back!
Round four is blasting off and you're invited to join the party. This year, the Helmut List Halle once again will transform into the ultimate roller disco - funky lights, stellar vibes, and plenty of room to glide. DJ Raifu will be spinning tracks to keep you grooving for hours, while Café Erde keeps you fueled with delicious food and drinks.
Look forward to cosmic surprises, exciting new features, and an event that's truly out of this world. Don’t miss it - it’s going to be a blast!

A nostalgic blast from the past for adults and thrilling adventure for kids: a classic Roller Disco just like the ones in the movies! This year's event promises to be even bigger and better with catering from Café Erde, music from the GRRRLS DJ crew and rental skates from the Austrian brand WIFA. Our all ages Open Disco goes from 3pm to 7pm and our 18+ Late Night Disco from 8pm to 12am. Whether you're a skate pro, a newbie or just want to join as a spectator - everyone is welcome at this unique event!

After our first event with over 400 guests and sold out tickets we’re back and ready for round two in the Helmut List Halle! Grab your skates and funky outfits and join us on this cosmic adventure through time and space. The Open Disco for all ages starts at 3pm and ends at 7pm. The Late Night Disco for all our 18+ guests goes from 8pm to 12am. Get ready to roll the night away to the best music and vibes this side of the Milky Way has to offer!

We are happy to present the first Space Quads Roller Disco in Graz! We invite you to join us on this spacey adventure, where we will skate, dance and party on eight wheels.
