Welcome to the unofficial Roller Skate Tricktionary

Here you can find an incomplete list of skatepark tricks you can do on 8 wheels, categorized by the difficulty rating

Whether you're stepping into a skatepark for the very first time or you're already comfortable dropping into ramps, this Tricktionary is here to help you discover new tricks and build your skills. Each trick is organized by difficulty so you can find challenges that match your current level and track your progress as you improve.

This is an unofficial, community-inspired resource created by roller skaters for roller skaters. The collection is always growing, so you may not find every trick here just yet. Grab your helmet, lace up your skates, and start exploring—your next favourite trick might be just a click away!

Featured Trick

Estro Kick

The Estro Kick is a stylish ramp trick where you momentarily lift and extend your legs while locked into a controlled position on the coping. It’s a confidence-heavy move that combines timing, balance, and commitment on transitions.

While it looks dramatic, it’s built on solid stall fundamentals—so if you’re comfortable with Soul Stalls and frontside stalls, this is a natural next step.
